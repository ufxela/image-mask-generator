Image Mask Generator

Upload a photo of a collage, automatically segment it into regions, select the pieces you want to highlight, and generate a black-and-white mask for projection.

How to Use:

  • Upload Image: Click "Choose Image" to upload a photo of your wall/collage
  • Adjust Sensitivity: Use the slider to control segmentation detail (lower = fewer, larger regions)
  • Segment: Click "Segment Image" to divide the image into selectable regions
  • Select Regions: Hover over regions to highlight them, click to select/deselect
  • Generate Mask: Click "Create Mask" to generate the black-and-white projection mask
  • Download: Click "Download Mask" to save the mask image
